Our Story

Established in 2023, Candy Creations was started by best friends Rebeca and Vanessa. They were in the market for custom healthy candy and they found nothing. At Candy Creations, we believe in real ingredients and no additives. Our mission is to create one of a kind candy that lives up to our wildest imaginations. Want candy in the shape of your face? We can do that! Want exotic flavors of candy with 24K gold flakes? We can do that too!
